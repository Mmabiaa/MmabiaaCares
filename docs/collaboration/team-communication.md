# Team Communication Guide

## Purpose
This document outlines the communication protocols, tools, and best practices for the MmabiaaCares development team to ensure effective collaboration and information sharing.

## Communication Channels

### 1. Synchronous Communication

#### Video Calls (Zoom/Google Meet)
- **When to use**:
  - Team meetings
  - Complex discussions
  - Brainstorming sessions
  - Sensitive conversations
- **Best Practices**:
  - Keep video on when possible
  - Use headphones in shared spaces
  - Mute when not speaking
  - Use the "raise hand" feature in large meetings

#### Instant Messaging (Slack)
- **Channels**:
  - `#general` - Announcements and important updates
  - `#development` - Technical discussions
  - `#random` - Non-work related conversations
  - `#alerts` - System alerts and monitoring
  - `#support` - User support and issues

- **Best Practices**:
  - Use threads for discussions
  - Use @channel and @here sparingly
  - Set your status when away
  - Use emoji reactions to reduce noise

### 2. Asynchronous Communication

#### Project Management (GitHub Projects/Jira)
- **When to use**:
  - Task tracking
  - Progress updates
  - Sprint planning
  - Bug tracking

#### Documentation (GitHub Wiki/Notion)
- **When to use**:
  - Technical documentation
  - Meeting notes
  - Decision records
  - Process documentation

## Meeting Schedule

### Regular Meetings

#### Daily Standup
- **When**: Monday-Friday, 10:00 AM EST
- **Duration**: 15 minutes
- **Format**:
  - What did you work on yesterday?
  - What are you working on today?
  - Any blockers?

#### Sprint Planning
- **When**: First Monday of sprint, 1:00 PM EST
- **Duration**: 1 hour
- **Purpose**: Plan work for the upcoming sprint

#### Sprint Review
- **When**: Last Friday of sprint, 2:00 PM EST
- **Duration**: 1 hour
- **Purpose**: Demo completed work to stakeholders

#### Retrospective
- **When**: Last Friday of sprint, 3:30 PM EST
- **Duration**: 45 minutes
- **Format**:
  - What went well?
  - What could be improved?
  - Action items for next sprint

### Ad Hoc Meetings
- Schedule with at least 1 hour notice
- Include an agenda in the invite
- Consider if the meeting could be an async update

## Communication Best Practices

### General Guidelines
- **Be clear and concise** - Get to the point quickly
- **Use the right channel** - Choose the most appropriate medium
- **Be respectful** - Consider different time zones and working hours
- **Document decisions** - Update relevant documentation after decisions are made

### Response Time Expectations
- **Urgent**: 1 hour (use phone call for critical issues)
- **High Priority**: 4 business hours
- **Normal**: 24 business hours
- **Low Priority**: 48 business hours

### Time Zone Considerations
- Team members are located in multiple time zones (EST, PST, GMT)
- Core collaboration hours: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM EST
- Respect non-working hours in team members' time zones

## Remote Work Guidelines

### Availability
- Set working hours in your calendar
- Update your status when stepping away
- Use Do Not Disturb when focusing

### Video Call Etiquette
- Test your setup before important calls
- Use a professional background or blur
- Be on time
- Have an agenda

### Asynchronous Work
- Document everything
- Assume good intent
- Be explicit about needs and deadlines

## Onboarding New Team Members

### First Week
1. Add to all relevant communication channels
2. Schedule 1:1s with team members
3. Review communication guidelines
4. Set up all necessary tools

### Buddy System
- Each new member is paired with a buddy
- Regular check-ins for the first month
- Go-to person for questions

## Tools and Resources

### Communication Tools
- **Slack**: Team chat
- **Zoom**: Video calls
- **Google Workspace**: Email and collaboration
- **GitHub**: Code and project management
- **Notion**: Documentation and wikis

### Best Practices by Tool

#### Slack
- Use threads for discussions
- Create channels for projects/topics
- Set notification preferences
- Use status updates

#### Email
- Clear subject lines
- Use bullet points for action items
- Keep it brief
- Use BCC appropriately

#### GitHub
- Use templates for issues and PRs
- Link related issues
- Use labels and milestones
- Keep discussions in the relevant place

## Handling Conflicts

### Conflict Resolution Process
1. **Direct Discussion**: Address issues one-on-one first
2. **Mediation**: Involve a team lead if needed
3. **Documentation**: Record the resolution
4. **Follow-up**: Check in after resolution

### Giving and Receiving Feedback
- Be specific and objective
- Focus on behavior, not personality
- Use "I" statements
- Be open to receiving feedback

## Emergency Procedures

### System Outages
1. Post in #alerts channel
2. Page on-call engineer
3. Create incident report
4. Conduct post-mortem

### Security Incidents
1. Follow security protocol
2. Contact security team immediately
3. Document all actions taken
4. Preserve evidence

## Continuous Improvement

### Feedback
- Regular team retrospectives
- Anonymous feedback form
- 1:1 meetings with managers

### Process Updates
- Review communication processes quarterly
- Update documentation as needed
- Train team on new tools/processes

## Appendix

### Communication Templates

#### Meeting Request
```
Subject: [Meeting] Topic - Date

Hi [Team],

I'd like to schedule a meeting to discuss [topic].

Proposed Time: [date/time]
Duration: [duration]
Location: [Zoom/Meeting Room]

Agenda:
1. [Agenda item 1]
2. [Agenda item 2]
3. [Agenda item 3]

Please let me know if this time works for you or suggest an alternative.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

#### Status Update
```
Subject: [Status] Project Name - Week of [date]

Hi [Team],

Here's an update on [project]:

✅ Completed:
- [Task 1]
- [Task 2]

🔄 In Progress:
- [Task 3] (ETA: [date])
- [Task 4] (Blocked by: [issue])

⏳ Up Next:
- [Task 5]
- [Task 6]

Blockers/Help Needed:
- [Description of issue]

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Common Abbreviations
- LGTM: Looks Good To Me
- WIP: Work In Progress
- TBD: To Be Determined
- EOD: End Of Day
- ETA: Estimated Time of Arrival/Completion
- FYI: For Your Information
- IMO/IMHO: In My (Honest) Opinion
- PR: Pull Request
- PTAL: Please Take A Look
- TYIA: Thank You In Advance

### Time Zone Reference
- EST: UTC-5 (UTC-4 during DST)
- PST: UTC-8 (UTC-7 during DST)
- GMT: UTC+0
